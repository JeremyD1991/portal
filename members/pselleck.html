Pete Selleck

Chairman & President of Michelin North America

As chairman and president of Michelin North America, Inc., Pete Selleck is responsible for coordinating all Michelin activities in the United States, Canada and Mexico. He holds degrees in engineering and applied sciences from the U.S. Military Academy at West Point and an MBA from Clemson University.

Pete has served on the boards of Junior Achievement of Greenville, United Way of Greenville County, the Greenville Technical College Foundation and the South Carolina Chamber of Commerce.
